SideNote

Get Extension

A simple note-taking extension for your browser's side panel.

SideNote allows you to quickly jot down notes, ideas, and to-do lists without leaving your current tab. It's perfect for students, researchers, and anyone who needs a simple way to stay organized.

Features:

  • 📝 Create and manage multiple notes
  • 🌗 Light and dark mode
  • 💾 Export and import your notes
  • ⌨️ Markdown support
